Lasagna

Homemade Lasagna is a classic that every cook should have in their rotation.

Tender sheets of pasta, a cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce make the perfect dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp. extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 2 lb. ground beef
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp. dried oregano
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 (32-0z.) jars marinara
  • 16 oz. whole milk ricotta
  • 1/2 c. freshly grated Parmesan, divided
  • 1/4 c. chopped parsley, plus more for garnish
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 lb. sliced mozzarella
